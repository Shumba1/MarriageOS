# T-004 — Deduplicate extracts and surface contradictions

## Objective
Merge overlapping extracts, remove duplication, and explicitly flag contradictions, drift points, and tension between source layers.

## Why it matters
MarriageOS cannot move downstream if repeated or conflicting doctrine is silently blended together.

## Inputs
- `sources/derived/extracts/`
- `docs/README_ACTIVE_SET.md`
- `docs/SOURCE_MANIFEST.md`
- `docs/MASTER_SPEC.md`
- `docs/DRIFT_REGISTER.md`
- `docs/pipeline/prompts/02-extract-deduper.md`

## Worker role
Driver

## Output path
- `sources/derived/extracts-deduped/`
- `docs/contradictions.md`

## Output format
Deduplicated extract files plus a contradiction ledger in markdown.

## Acceptance criteria
- obvious duplicates are merged
- merged extracts preserve source lineage
- contradictions are logged explicitly
- unresolved tensions are not silently decided unless canonical authority clearly resolves them
- canonical wins are recorded where conflict exists
- deduped extract set is materially cleaner than the raw extract layer

## Observability hooks
- deduped_extract_count
- duplicate_cluster_count
- contradiction_count
- canonical_override_count
- unresolved_contradiction_count
- dedupe_pass_complete = true/false

## Status
inbox

## Notes
If a contradiction cannot be resolved from canonical authority, leave it open and log it explicitly.
